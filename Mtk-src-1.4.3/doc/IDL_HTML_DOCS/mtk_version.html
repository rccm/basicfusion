-Abstract

   MTK_VERSION reports MISR Toolkit version.

-Copyright

   U.S. Government sponsorship acknowledged.

-I/O
   
   the call:
   
      print, MTK_VERSION()
   
-Examples

      ;;
      ;; The call
      ;;
      print, MTK_VERSION()
      
   IDL outputs:
   
      1.2.0

-Particulars
   None.

-Required Reading

   the MTK routine MtkVersion.c.
